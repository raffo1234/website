Our Services

LICENSING

We offer attention-to-detail service by licensing the use of any of our songs, and assisting on contacting the master owner.

Through our personalized service of our dedicated music-licensing specialist we ensure you get licenses quickly and easily for TV Shows, films, commercial or whatever other project you have in mind.

CUSTOMIZED VERSIONS

If you are not interested on using the original master, we provide you with personalize versions of any of the songs or an original music.

If you are interested in a song and want to contact us about a license just send an email to contact@elixpublishing.com or give us a call at (305)994-4999 and we will help you start the process.

ORIGINAL MUSIC

Wecompose and produce music for every possible media application: musical arrangements and recordings, original soundtracks, re-recordings.

Elix Publishing Inc. represents almost the totality of the music catalog of Rafael Pérez-Botija. It contains more than 200 songs written from the 70’s to the present. Many of which have been great hits in the record industry performed by phenomenal artists such as Jose Jose, RocioDurcal, Enrique Iglesias, Camilo Sesto, Manuel Mijares, Cristian Castro, Jay-z, David Bustamante, Julio Iglesias, Herb Alpert, Lolita, Parchís, Mocedades, Lucero, India, Chayanne, Masiel, Dulce, YolanditaMonje, LupitaDálessio, Lucia Méndez, Alex Sintec, Valeria Lynch, Molotov, Pablo Abraira, Sergio Fachelliand many others.

Our goal is to connect our compositions with as many people as possible whether via TV, movies, merchandise or the many other outlets for music.
